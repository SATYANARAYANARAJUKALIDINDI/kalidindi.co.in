About Us

We are Orbicular, a dynamic fully diversified speciality pharmaceutical company setting standards of excellence in product development.

As a speciality company we deliver quality pharmaceutical solutions by resolving technological complexity, create expedited regulatory filing strategies, provide solutions to market access barriers and design products that meet unmet medical need demands, in the complex generic and specialty pharmaceutical space.

We empower healthcare and improve lives of patients by providing affordability and timely access of quality generic and speciality medicines as a substitute of very expensive branded drugs
